Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987 in the city of Rosario.
His grandmother brought him to football and because dedicates all his goals to him.
His first team was Barcelona then PSG and now he plays for Inter Miami.
He has won a lot of titles, especially cool is 8 Ballon d'Or, which is an all-time record, and 1 World Cup.
